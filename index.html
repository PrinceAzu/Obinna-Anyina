Who am I ?

Public Speaker | Stage Craftsman

Obinna Anyina, popularly known in the public-speaking circles as "Obi X da Voice" is a seasoned compere, public speaker, events commentator and stage craftsman with close to two decades of experience in driving audience engagements in both the public and private sectors.

He has anchored numerous, high-profile events for the Nigerian Presidency, the Central Bank of Nigeria, notable local and foreign brands, as well as major promotional gigs across the corporate spectrum in Nigeria. He was the moderator at the signing of a tripartite agreement on Financial Inclusion between the Federal Government of Nigeria and the Kingdom of the Netherlands, an event that had in attendance, the then Princess Maxima of the Netherlands, eFINA and key stakeholders in the financial system.

Obinna is currently a Principal Manager in the Corporate Communications Department of the Central Bank of Nigeria where he Heads the Internal & Branch Communications Office of the Department. He produced the first-ever audio version of the CBN Act 2007 as a contributory initiative to the information assets of Nigeria's apex bank and has had his voice on the audiovisual marketing projects of a plethora of local and foreign brands.
